Asian Greens

Description

Restaurant-style Asian greens are so easy to achieve at home and they take next to no time to prepare and cook.

Slice any greens you can find in store – bok choy, gai larn (Chinese broccoli), broccolini, choy sum – and stir-fry with garlic and oyster sauce in a wok.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 6 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 300g (10oz) Asian greens, sliced
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok over high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and stir-fry for half a minute.
  3. Add the greens and oyster sauce and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes or until just tender but still crisp.
