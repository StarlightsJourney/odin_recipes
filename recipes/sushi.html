Japanese Sushi

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients , such as seafood—often raw—and vegetables. Styles of sushi and its presentation vary widely, but the one key ingredient is "sushi rice", also referred to as shari, or sumeshi.

Ingredients

  • Japanese Rice
  • Fillings of your choice
  • Nori Sheets
  • Bamboo Sushi Mat

Steps

  1. Steam the rice before seasoning with sushi vinegar.
  2. For this example, we'll use cucumber. Slice the ingredient into strips.
  3. Using half a sheet of nori, place a blob of rice and add some strips of cucumber before rolling it up with the mat.
